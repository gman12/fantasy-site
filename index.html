Welcome!

If you like fantastical worlds, dangerous beasts, perilous warriors,
and glorious action and adventure, then you've come to the right place! So have
fun exploring the world of Swords for the Ages, and remember, sometimes the
best worlds are the ones we create ourselves!

About the Author

My name is Gaurav Sirdeshpande. I am currently a college student studying Computer
Science. I also consider myself a budding writer, and Swords for the Ages is my first
attempt at writing a full-fledged novel. When I am not writing or programming, you can probably
find me playing tennis, piano, eating delicious food (avocados have a special place in my heart),
or spending time with my friends and family.
