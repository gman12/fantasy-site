Characters

There are a lot of characters in Swords for the Ages. Here
are some of the most important ones.

Major Characters

Raymar Finariel
The son of Lord Oraphoro Finariel, and the nephew of Toralin Finariel,
King of Valindor and the Highlands, Prince Raymar Finariel is a headstrong young
man who is adept in the arts of both war and magic. Despite his skills, he is
still inexperienced, and prone to rash and immature decisions.

Proteus
A giant of a man, his strength and combat prowess is matched only by his unflinching
loyalty to his charge, Raymar Finariel, whome he has known since both were children. While he takes
his job very seriously, he is generally a jovial man of many jokes. He inherited his fantastic
combat skills from his father and uncle, who are famed warriors themselves, and is especially dangerous
with his massive warhammer.

Blain Orycinth
The captain of the guard of House Favriel, and close friend of Lord Genneth
Favriel. A centaur veteran of many wars and battles, he is a noble and proud warrior,
always trying to care for the lives of those around him.

Dareth of Granmire
One of the last Panorians in Renvieln, Dareth Finariel is originally from the land
to the south known as Darross, where he was a memeber of the Order of the Rainbow, a prestigious
order of mystics who controlled the various elements. He then went on to serve Lady Aisha, before
accompanying her to Renvieln after she married the Elven lord Quentin Fairwood, whom he served thereafter.
Dareth is a powerful elemental warrior, with an expert control over the element of fire.

Lea Fairwood
The daughter of Lord Quentin Fairwood, and heir to the Elven realm of Silvervale, Lea Fairwood
is equally perilous as she is beautiful. An immeasurably proficient archer, she is also equally talented
with magic. After spending her childhood in Silvervale, she moved to Mithral Erith as a ward of King Toralin
where she befriended Raymar Finariel.

Egen More
The son of Rhogen More, lord of the vast underground kingdom of Boldlind, Egen More is a gruff, no-nonsense
dwarf who relishes the chance to use his fearsome battle-axe. Despite his short stature, he is never one to back down
from a fight, although his lack of control over his loud mouth can sometimes put him in difficult situations. He
spent much of his young adulthood in Gleavenford, where he formed a fast friendship with Captain Blain Orycinth.
